Overview

LPK Investment Group is a global alternative asset management firm headquartered in New York. The firm was founded in by pooling expertise from key senior partners with an impressive investment track record spanning the past 20+ years. As a small and focused firm, LPK has continued to provide outstanding return and investment services to our clients since inception.

The firm was founded with the goal of providing investment management services to selected clients through a disciplined and multi-faceted investment approach with a keen focus on investment performance, operational transparency, risk management, and client services.
